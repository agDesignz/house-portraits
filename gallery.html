I have a dear friend, Michael, for whom I wanted to get a special gift so I immediately thought of Alex Geer and his beautiful house portraits. Alex's pen and ink drawing of the home was beautifully rendered, but just as importantly, it was warm and personal.

--Diane, Massachusetts

Each portrait is drawn on archival quality paper, with archival ink. Both paper and ink are acid-free. They also look great on stationary, coffee mugs, mouse pads or even a face masks.

Whether framed and hanging on the wall, or printed on an accessory, a house portrait makes a great gift for holidays, weddings, or any occasion. And don't forget house-warming gifts.
